Copy the 3 folders (Cores/, Platforms/, Assets/) to the root of your Analogue Pocket SD card.

Note: macOS Finder replaces folders instead of merging them so do it all manually and be careful.

Place your ROMs and gba_bios.bin in /Assets/gba/common/

Analogizer builds are provided as a separate core under Cores/mincer_ray.GBA_Analogizer/.
Use that core when connecting the Analogizer-FPGA adapter.
